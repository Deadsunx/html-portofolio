My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul is a dark fantasy anime series based on the manga of the same name. It follows Ken Kaneki, a college student who becomes a half-ghoul after a chance encounter with one. The series explores themes of identity, humanity, and the struggle between ghouls and humans.

All The Mission Impossible

The Mission: Impossible series is a high-octane action film franchise centered around Ethan Hunt, an agent of the Impossible Mission Force (IMF). Known for its thrilling stunts, espionage plots, and intricate heists, the series has become iconic in the action genre.
